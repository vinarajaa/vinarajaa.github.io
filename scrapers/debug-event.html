SALON DU CHOCOLAT NYC 2026

By Salon du Chocolat New York

Overview

A unique global event celebrating the world of chocolate in the heart of New York City! Open to chocolate lovers, families, chefs & buyers.

Dates & Location:

📅 March 7 - 8, 2026

📍 415 Fifth Ave. (@ 37th Street) New York, NY 100​​16


Event schedule:*
Saturday: 10:00 AM-6:00 PM

Sunday: 10:00 AM-5:00 PM

*Hours are subject to change

Discover the Magic of Chocolate Like Never Before

Step into a world of indulgence where chocolate takes center stage! From artisan confections and cultural craftsmanship to interactive experiences and sweet surprises, this immersive event is expertly curated for chocolate lovers of all ages.

Come solo, plan a unique date, bring your friends, or enjoy family fun — whether you're gifting, tasting, or simply savoring the moment, there's something delicious for everyone!

Taste your way through a curated world of chocolate and confections in March, 2026.

What’s in Store?

Live Chef Demos & Seminars: Watch world-renowned chocolatiers create show-stopping treats right before your eyes. Plus, join engaging seminars led by experts in the chocolate industry as they share insights, techniques, trends, and beyond.

Choco-Lover’s Paradise: Shop gourmet chocolates, unique gifts, and luxurious confections.

Chocolate Fashion & Live Sculpting: Marvel at couture and sculptures made entirely of chocolate — an unforgettable feast for the eyes.

Bean-to-Bar Journey: Explore the origins of chocolate and see how it’s made by top artisan makers.

Bite-Sized Kid’s Zone: Interactive chocolate fun for the little ones — a family favorite!

Interactive Photo Ops: Play, pose, and immerse yourself in the sweetest content moments.


And that’s just the begnning — with much more to come, stay tuned for exciting announcements and full schedules online at www.salonduchocolatnyc.com

Are there age restrictions?

Salon du Chocolat NYC is an event for all ages. Adult supervision is required for minors under 18. Age requirements may be in effect for certain onsite activities.

Who needs a ticket?

All attendees must purchase a ticket, even if it's free.

  • Free for Children under 6 (must register)
  • Children (6-12): Requires a child ticket
  • Adults (13+): Requires a general admission ticket

Proof of age may be required onsite. Minors under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian.

By ensuring every attendee has purchased the appropriate ticket, we can guarantee a smooth registration process and an enjoyable experience for everyone at Salon du Chocolat NYC.


Taxes and Other Important Info

No refunds or exchanges are permitted. Taxes and other fees will be added to your ticket purchase during checkout. Ticket prices may be higher on-site. Purchase ahead of time to save!

*Ticket Prices reflect pre-sale rates and are subject to change.


Notes

  • Tickets are non-refundable and cannot be exchanged.
  • All purchases are final and cannot be modified.
